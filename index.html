Programming Philosophy

Function before simplicity and simplicity before beauty. Now this doesn't mean I don't use all three aspects, it is simply my workflow. Secondly I also like to stay flexible with what language to use and when. I learn quickly why a language functions the way it does, then the what and how of a language falls into place. I research a project to find all available possible documentation to add to my pallet of tools to work from. Knowledge is power and this is especially true in programming.

What Languages does your team need?

Instead of being highly specialized in any one or two languages. I will continue to learn as many languages as I possibly can. A combination of languages is usually what makes the coding machine run at it's best. I absorb programming language and styles like a sponge and am as malleable enough to adjust to a team's needs.
